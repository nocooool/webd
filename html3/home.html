Taco Trivia

For how long tacos have been around ??

How long? Well, some believe the taco was invented between 1000 and 500 B.C, although the word 'taco' originated from Mexican silver miners in the 18th century. At the time it was used to describe the thin sheets of paper wrapped in gunpowder that was used as an explosive, although the miners did enjoy a more flavorsome alternative as part of their staple diet.
